− Our story −

We are a small team of people with a big passion for tasty salty pies and simple, healthy cake. We love tasty recipes, those simple recipes in which you can easily identify high value, but delicate, subtle ingredients. This place was born out of our wish to offer everyone access to the recipes we cook at home for our family or for special occasions. All our recipes are made here, with lots of love, care, attention and passion. We love chocolate, we love dairy products, like organic cheese, yogurt, and localy produced fruits and vegetables, but more than that we love to combine them into tasty and healthy recipes. We wish that our passion brings added value to the recipes we are used to and also wish that you will find, in each recipe healthy and nutritious ingredients, season fruits and vegetables, dairy products from local farms, and as much as possible bio products. We do not use artificial food colorings, neither food improvers, nor any taste enhancers. We always opened to our customer’s wish, we are here to make your life tastier!
